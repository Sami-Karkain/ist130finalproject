+# Neon Courier
+
+A browser-based cyber-arcade runner where you guide a rogue courier through neon-lit districts to deliver encrypted data while uncovering Helix Megacorp's secrets.
+
+## Play
+
+Open `index.html` in a modern browser. Use **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to move. Hold **E** to hack barriers, dodge drones, and collect energy nodes to reveal the megacorp conspiracy. Grab all six nodes and hack both barriers to complete a run; press **R** anytime for a quick restart.
+
